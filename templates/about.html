About Urban Sofas

Our Story

Urban Sofas began with a mission: to make quality, locally crafted furniture accessible to everyone in Nairobi. We combine style, comfort, and affordability to help you create a home you truly love.

From contemporary sofas to elegant dining sets, our products reflect a blend of Kenyan craftsmanship and modern design sensibility — built to last and impress.

Our Selection

Our curated range includes plush sectionals, versatile love seats, stylish dining sets, and premium beds. Each piece is selected for its design, comfort, and durability — ensuring it fits seamlessly into your home.

We’re committed to helping you find furniture that reflects your lifestyle, whether you’re decorating a new space or upgrading your current one.
